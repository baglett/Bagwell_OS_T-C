Privacy Policy

Bagwell OS SMS Service

Effective Date: March 8, 2026

1. Introduction

Bagwell OS (“we”, “us”, “our”) is a personal AI assistant that communicates via SMS. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. This service is operated by a single individual for personal use and is not offered commercially.

2. Information We Collect

When you interact with Bagwell OS via SMS, we collect:

  • Phone number — your mobile phone number as provided by Twilio in E.164 format
  • Message content — the text of SMS messages you send to and receive from the service
  • Message metadata — timestamps, message identifiers, and delivery status

We do not collect any additional personal information beyond what is transmitted through the SMS interaction.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Process and respond to your SMS messages
  • Manage tasks, reminders, and notifications you request
  • Send daily digest summaries and scheduled alerts you configure
  • Maintain conversation history for context in future interactions

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored on a privately owned server running on a local network that is not directly accessible from the public internet
  • The database is secured with authentication credentials and is not exposed to external networks
  • All webhook communications between Twilio and our server are encrypted via HTTPS and validated using HMAC-SHA1 signature verification

5. Third-Party Services

We use Twilio as our SMS messaging provider. Twilio processes your phone number and message content to deliver SMS messages. Twilio’s privacy policy is available at twilio.com/legal/privacy.

We do not sell, rent, share, or disclose your personal information to any other third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

6. Data Retention

Message history and conversation data are retained indefinitely for the purpose of maintaining assistant context. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting the service operator.

7. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Opt out — text STOP to the service number to stop receiving all messages
  • Request data deletion — contact the service operator to have your data removed
  • Request data access — contact the service operator to obtain a copy of your stored data

8. Children’s Privacy

This service is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

10. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your data rights, contact the service operator via SMS to the Bagwell OS phone number, or open an issue on the GitHub repository.
